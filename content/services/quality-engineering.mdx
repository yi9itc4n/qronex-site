## EN

### What We Do

We design and improve quality systems that scale. From APQP and PPAP readiness to layered process audits and SPC, we build reliable, data-driven practices that reduce variation and elevate performance.

### Key Capabilities

- Process mapping and bottleneck analysis
- Control plan and FMEA enhancement
- SPC setup and dashboarding
- Problem solving (8D, QRQC) enablement
- Readiness for IATF/ISO requirements

### Outcomes

- Shorter issue resolution time
- Lower defect rates and rework
- Clear governance and reporting
