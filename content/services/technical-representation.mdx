## EN

### What We Do

We act as your on-site quality interface with OEMs and Tier1s. We run daily routines, escalate the right topics, and coordinate immediate containment with structured follow-up until closure.

### Key Capabilities

- On-site customer interface and reporting
- Daily QRQC/8D routines and escalation
- Containment leadership and shopfloor presence
- Launch and ramp-up quality support

### Outcomes

- Faster decisions and clearer communication
- Minimal disruption at the customer
- Stable routines and documented learnings
